## Illustration 1

**Position**: After "#### 1. 0 或 1 的残酷游戏"
**Purpose**: Contrast the continuous nature of Traditional Finance with the binary nature of Prediction Markets.
**Visual Content**:
- **Left (Traditional)**: A stock chart moving in waves. Label: "连续市场 (Continuous) - 线性盈亏".
- **Right (Prediction)**: A chart splitting into two distinct paths at the end: Flat 1.0 or Flat 0.0. Label: "二元市场 (Binary) - 0 或 1".
